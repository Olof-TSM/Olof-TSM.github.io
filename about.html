About Victor Ramsay

Victor Ramsay is one of the worlds most renown chefs. Following in the footsteps of culinary genius Gordon Ramsay, Victor has had a large impact on the culinary community through his innovative recipes and Videos. Victor Ramsay started out his journey in grade 9 where he discovered his love for cooking during a personal interest project. Since then, he has continued to develop and improve his skills and techniques. This website was created to share his recipes with other aspiring chefs.
